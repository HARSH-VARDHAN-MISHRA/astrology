My Story

Greetings!

I am a tarot reader, trainer, and counselor who utilizes positive psychology to assist individuals in enhancing the quality of their lives. Having been born and raised in Delhi, India, I have always felt a deep connection to spirituality and harbored a curiosity about numbers and human psychology.

In terms of education, I pursued an MBA in Human Resource and worked in that field for a few years. However, I found little joy in my professional journey. In 2013, I made the pivotal decision to leave my job and embark on a full-fledged spiritual journey. In essence, I believe that I was chosen by a higher power.

In creating Spirituall Lifestyle , our aim is to provide a sacred space where individuals can explore and enhance their spiritual journey. Our curated selection of crystals, bracelets, and jewelry is designed to bring positive energy, mindfulness, and balance into your life. Ordering from us means investing in quality items that resonate with intention, aiding you on your path to self-discovery and tranquility.

My core belief centers around maintaining an attitude of gratitude and focusing on counting our blessings rather than our problems. Shifting our perspective from challenges to blessings can significantly ease the journey of life .

I have been a guiding light for numerous individuals and have dedicated a decade to contributing to the field of occult science. I am enthusiastic about continuing to be a beacon of guidance for others through my programs.
