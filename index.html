My Story

Greetings!

I am a tarot reader, trainer, and counselor who utilizes positive psychology to assist individuals in enhancing the quality of their lives. Having been born and raised in Delhi, India, I have always felt a deep connection to spirituality and harbored a curiosity about numbers and human psychology.

In terms of education, I pursued an MBA in Human Resource and worked in that field for a few years. However, I found little joy in my professional journey. In 2013, I made the pivotal decision to leave my job and embark on a full-fledged spiritual journey. In essence, I believe that I was chosen by a higher power.

In creating Spirituall Lifestyle , our aim is to provide a sacred space where individuals can explore and enhance their spiritual journey. Our curated selection of crystals, bracelets, and jewelry is designed to bring positive energy, mindfulness, and balance into your life. Ordering from us means investing in quality items that resonate with intention, aiding you on your path to self-discovery and tranquility.

My core belief centers around maintaining an attitude of gratitude and focusing on counting our blessings rather than our problems. Shifting our perspective from challenges to blessings can significantly ease the journey of life .

I have been a guiding light for numerous individuals and have dedicated a decade to contributing to the field of occult science. I am enthusiastic about continuing to be a beacon of guidance for others through my programs.

Our Services

Numerology

Numerology explores the mystical connection between numbers and one's life events. By assigning meaning to numbers derived from your birthdate and name, it aims to reveal your personality, strengths, and even predict your future to help you make better life choices

Tarot Card

Tarot cards, more than just fortune telling tools, are a symbolic map for navigating life's journey. Divided into Major Arcana (archetypal forces) and Minor Arcana (everyday situations), these 78 cards offer guidance and insights when interpreted by a reader

Law of Attraction

The Law of Attraction is a belief that positive thoughts draw positive experiences, and vice versa. By focusing on your desires and maintaining a hopeful outlook, you can supposedly attract them into your life with ease through our divine guidance and support

Switch Words

Switch words are short, powerful words believed to tap into your subconscious mind. By saying them repeatedly, you can supposedly influence your energy and bring about positive changes in your life, like attracting wealth or improving relationships

Cosmic Healing

Explore the world of energy healing! Chakras, believed to be energy centers in your body, and Reiki, a technique to balance them using universal energy. Discover how this practice may promote well-being and guide you towards a more balanced you

Angel Card

Ever wonder if angels have messages for you? Angel cards offer uplifting guidance and insights. Simply shuffle the deck, ask a question, and draw a card for an inspiring message from the angelic realm of beautiful imagery and simple interpretations
